Experiences

Beyond the Room

Palagya is more than a place to sleep. It is a venue for celebration, a space for gathering, and a rooftop from which to watch the Kathmandu valley breathe.

500-Seat Banquet Hall

Weddings, conferences, and celebrations in a grand space that honors Nepali hospitality at scale.
